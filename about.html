Design & privacy

Senior Sync is designed around four core principles:

  1. Privacy-first: Keep processing on-device and avoid storing raw audio in the cloud.
  2. Respect & consent: Explicit permissions and caregiver opt-in.
  3. Low false alarms: Multi-sensor fusion and gentle verification before escalation.
  4. Accessibility: Clear voice prompts, large UI targets, and contrast for older eyes.

Prototype notes

This web prototype demonstrates interaction patterns and caregiver flows. A production rollout requires clinical validation, secure alerting, and compliance with local regulations.
